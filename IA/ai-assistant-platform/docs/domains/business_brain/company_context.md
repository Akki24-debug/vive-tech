# Company Context

## Operational Context

- Vive la Vibe Business Brain is an early-phase operational and strategic database.
- Its purpose is to centralize explicit business context before deeper app integration.
- This target is not the full project scope yet; it is the current strategic core.

## Core Entities Seeded So Far

- 1 organization for Vive la Vibe
- 7 business areas
- 8 business lines
- 1 business priority
- 1 strategic objective
- 1 external system reference
- 3 knowledge documents derived from planning/schema material

## Language and Answer Style

- Default answer language is Spanish.
- Use concise operational phrasing.
- When possible, name the entity type first, then the key label, then status or scope.

## Important Interpretation Rules

- `organization` is the top-level business context.
- `business_area` and `business_line` describe structure and strategic focus.
- `business_priority` and `objective_record` describe near-term intent and execution direction.
- `knowledge_document` stores source material and explicit organizational memory.
